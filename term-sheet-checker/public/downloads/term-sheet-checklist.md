# The 25-Point Term Sheet Red Flag Checklist

**VentureCounsel.AI | venturecounsel.ai**

---

Use this checklist every time you receive a term sheet. Check each item and flag anything that needs discussion with your lawyer or negotiation with investors.

---

## SECTION 1: ECONOMICS (Check First)

### 1. Pre-Money Valuation
- [ ] Valuation is clearly stated
- [ ] I understand if option pool is included in pre-money (it usually is)
- [ ] Effective valuation (after option pool) is acceptable

**Red Flag:** Unusually large option pool (>15%) carved from pre-money

### 2. Investment Amount
- [ ] Total round size is clear
- [ ] I know who is investing how much
- [ ] Lead investor is identified

### 3. Liquidation Preference
- [ ] Preference is 1x (standard)
- [ ] Non-participating (standard)
- [ ] No unusual seniority provisions

**Red Flag:** Participating preferred, 2x+ preference, or senior to other investors

### 4. Anti-Dilution Protection
- [ ] Broad-based weighted average (standard)
- [ ] Clear carve-outs for option grants and strategic issuances

**Red Flag:** Full ratchet or narrow-based weighted average

### 5. Dividends
- [ ] Non-cumulative (standard) or no dividends
- [ ] If cumulative, rate is reasonable (<8%)

**Red Flag:** Cumulative dividends above 8%

---

## SECTION 2: CONTROL & GOVERNANCE

### 6. Board Composition
- [ ] Founder majority or balanced board at seed
- [ ] Clear definition of who appoints each seat
- [ ] Independent seat selection process is fair

**Red Flag:** Investor majority at seed stage

### 7. Protective Provisions (Investor Veto Rights)
- [ ] Limited to major transactions (sale, new equity, debt)
- [ ] No veto over hiring/firing decisions
- [ ] No veto over day-to-day operations
- [ ] Thresholds are reasonable

**Red Flag:** Approval needed for executive compensation, customer contracts, or budgets

### 8. Voting Rights
- [ ] Preferred votes on as-converted basis (standard)
- [ ] No special super-voting rights

### 9. Drag-Along Rights
- [ ] Requires both preferred AND common majority
- [ ] Minimum price floor exists
- [ ] Same terms for all shareholders

**Red Flag:** Can be triggered by preferred alone at any price

---

## SECTION 3: FOUNDER TERMS

### 10. Founder Vesting
- [ ] Credit for time already served
- [ ] Vesting period is reasonable (2-4 years remaining)
- [ ] Cliff is waived or reasonable

**Red Flag:** Full 4-year restart with 1-year cliff

### 11. Acceleration
- [ ] Double-trigger acceleration on change of control
- [ ] At least 50% acceleration on qualifying termination

**Red Flag:** No acceleration provisions

### 12. Non-Compete/Non-Solicit
- [ ] Reasonable duration (12 months or less)
- [ ] Geographic scope is limited
- [ ] Clear exceptions for future activities

**Red Flag:** Unlimited duration or worldwide scope

### 13. IP Assignment
- [ ] Standard assignment of work product
- [ ] Carve-outs for prior inventions are clear

---

## SECTION 4: INVESTOR RIGHTS

### 14. Pro-Rata Rights
- [ ] Limited to lead/major investors
- [ ] Minimum threshold defined
- [ ] Not unlimited or "super pro-rata"

### 15. Information Rights
- [ ] Quarterly financials (not monthly)
- [ ] Annual audited statements reasonable
- [ ] No real-time dashboard access required
- [ ] Investor threshold for rights is appropriate

**Red Flag:** Monthly detailed financials for all investors

### 16. Registration Rights
- [ ] Standard S-3 registration rights
- [ ] Reasonable demand registration limits
- [ ] Customary lockup provisions

### 17. Right of First Refusal (ROFR)
- [ ] Company has right to repurchase first
- [ ] Investors have secondary right
- [ ] Carve-outs for estate planning

---

## SECTION 5: PROCESS TERMS

### 18. No-Shop Period
- [ ] 30-45 days (standard)
- [ ] Clear termination triggers
- [ ] Ends automatically if financing doesn't close

**Red Flag:** 60+ days or no termination provisions

### 19. Closing Conditions
- [ ] Reasonable and achievable
- [ ] No unusual due diligence requirements
- [ ] Timeline is clear

### 20. Expenses
- [ ] Investor legal fees are capped
- [ ] Cap is reasonable ($15-25K for seed)

**Red Flag:** Uncapped legal fees or cap above $50K at seed

---

## SECTION 6: RED FLAGS TO WALK AWAY FROM

### 21. Participating Preferred
- [ ] NOT included (this is a major red flag)

### 22. Full Ratchet Anti-Dilution
- [ ] NOT included (this is a major red flag)

### 23. Redemption Rights
- [ ] NOT included (this is a major red flag)

### 24. Multiple Liquidation Preferences
- [ ] NOT included (anything above 1x is a red flag)

### 25. Pay-to-Play Without Protection
- [ ] If included, terms protect founders in down rounds

---

## NEGOTIATION PRIORITY GUIDE

**Fight Hard For:**
1. 1x non-participating liquidation preference
2. Broad-based weighted average anti-dilution
3. Founder acceleration provisions
4. Reasonable no-shop period

**Negotiate If Aggressive:**
1. Board composition
2. Protective provision scope
3. Information rights frequency
4. Expense caps

**Usually Accept:**
1. Standard pro-rata rights for lead investors
2. Standard ROFR provisions
3. Customary registration rights
4. Reasonable vesting (with credit for time served)

---

## QUESTIONS TO ASK YOUR LAWYER

1. "What's the effective valuation after the option pool?"
2. "Walk me through the liquidation waterfall at 2x and 5x exit"
3. "Which protective provisions affect day-to-day operations?"
4. "What triggers the anti-dilution adjustment?"
5. "What happens to my unvested stock if I'm terminated?"
