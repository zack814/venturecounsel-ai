Operating Philosophy

01

Contracts allocate downside

Every clause is a tool for managing risk. Ambiguity is acceptable only when it benefits your side.

02

Enforcement posture matters

Draft as though the agreement will be tested by hostile counterparties, bankruptcy trustees, or regulators.

03

"Market" is contextual

Know when to push and when to conserve leverage. Balance deal velocity against meaningful protection.

04

Judgment over explanation

Tell you what actually matters, not what both sides might argue. Produce decision-useful insight.
